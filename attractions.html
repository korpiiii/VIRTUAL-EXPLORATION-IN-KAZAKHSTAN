Discover Kazakhstan

Explore the breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Kazakhstan.

Top 5 Must-Visit Places

Charyn Canyon

Known as the "Grand Canyon of Kazakhstan." Perfect for hiking and photography.

Baikonur Cosmodrome

The world’s largest space launch facility where Yuri Gagarin was launched.

Medeu Skating Rink

The highest ice skating rink, surrounded by beautiful mountains.

Big Almaty Lake

A breathtaking turquoise lake, ideal for hiking and photography.

Turkestan

A UNESCO site with stunning Persian-style architecture.

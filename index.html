Geography of Kazakhstan

Did You Know?

Kazakhstan is the largest landlocked country in the world, covering an area of approximately 2.7 million square kilometers. It is located in Central Asia, bordered by Russia, China, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, and Turkmenistan.

The country features vast steppes, mountains, deserts, and lakes, including the famous Caspian Sea, the Tien Shan mountains, and Lake Balkhash. Kazakhstan's climate is continental, with hot summers and cold winters.

Top 5 Must-Visit Places in Kazakhstan

Charyn Canyon

Known as the "Grand Canyon of Kazakhstan." Perfect for hiking and photography.

Baikonur Cosmodrome

The world’s largest space launch facility where Yuri Gagarin was launched.

Medeu Skating Rink

The highest ice skating rink, surrounded by beautiful mountains.

Big Almaty Lake

A breathtaking turquoise lake, ideal for hiking and photography.

Turkestan

A UNESCO site with stunning Persian-style architecture.

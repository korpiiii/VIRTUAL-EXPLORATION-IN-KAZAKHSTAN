Geography of Kazakhstan

Did You Know?

Kazakhstan is the largest landlocked country in the world, covering an area of approximately 2.7 million square kilometers. It is located in Central Asia, bordered by Russia, China, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, and Turkmenistan.

The country features vast steppes, mountains, deserts, and lakes, including the famous Caspian Sea, the Tien Shan mountains, and Lake Balkhash. Kazakhstan's climate is continental, with hot summers and cold winters.
